关于我

About Me

我是一名专业咖啡师，拥有SCA（精品咖啡协会）认证。这里记录了我对咖啡技艺的深度探索， 以及我对咖啡科学的专业理解。希望通过这个网站，能够与更多咖啡专业人士分享交流，推广精品咖啡文化。

I am a professional barista with SCA (Specialty Coffee Association) certification. Here I record my deep exploration of coffee techniques, as well as my professional understanding of coffee science. I hope to share and communicate with more coffee professionals through this website, promoting specialty coffee culture.
